Environment Map Lighting Modes

A first pass at the two-mode split: one for physically based rendering, one for editor-style model inspection.

PBR Environment

HDR map drives diffuse irradiance and prefiltered specular. Use this when checking material response, reflections, final screenshots, and realistic exposure.

HDR IBL active Reflections matter Material validation

Design Editor Lighting

Stable neutral viewport lighting for model reading, closer to Revit or Blender solid/material preview. Geometry should stay legible even when the HDR map is dark or high contrast.
